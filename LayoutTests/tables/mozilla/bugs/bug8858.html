Welcome to Pacific Shore Properties
Pacific Shore Properties is situated on beautiful Mayne Island.

Mayne Island is one of the southern Gulf Islands, off the coast of British Columbia, Canada, between Vancouver and Vancouver Island.
(See map: left)

Pacific Shore Properties specializes in the diverse properties of the Southern Gulf Islands; which include Beach, Business, View, Acreage, Home, Retirement and Recreation.

In business for over 35 years, and the oldest real estate company on Mayne Island, Pacific Shore Properties knowledge and expertise on the Gulf Islands is unmatched.
