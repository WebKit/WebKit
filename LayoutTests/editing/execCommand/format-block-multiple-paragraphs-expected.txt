This tests ensures formatBlock do not make multiple elements when formatting multiple paragraphs.

Formatting:
| "\n"
| <div>
|   "<#selection-anchor>hello"
| <div>
|   "world"
| <div>
|   "WebKit<#selection-focus>"
| "\n"

by p yields:
| "\n"
| <p>
|   "<#selection-anchor>hello"
|   <br>
|   "world"
|   <br>
|   "WebKit<#selection-focus>"
| "\n"
